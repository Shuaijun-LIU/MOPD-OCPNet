Citation for Combined Ocean Environment Dataset
==============================================

Please cite this dataset using one of the following formats:

APA Style (7th Edition):
------------------------
Author, A., et al. (2024). Combined Ocean Environment Dataset [Data set]. Ocean Research Institute. https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx

Chicago Style (17th Edition):
----------------------------
Author, A., et al. 2024. "Combined Ocean Environment Dataset." Data set. Ocean Research Institute. https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx.

MLA Style (9th Edition):
-----------------------
Author, A., et al. "Combined Ocean Environment Dataset." 2024. Ocean Research Institute, https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx.

BibTeX:
-------
@dataset{author2024combined,
  author       = {Author, A. and Others},
  title        = {Combined Ocean Environment Dataset},
  year         = {2024},
  publisher    = {Ocean Research Institute},
  doi          = {10.xxxx/xxxxx},
  url          = {https://example.com/dataset}
}
